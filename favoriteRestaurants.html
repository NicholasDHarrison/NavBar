My Five Current Favorite Restaurants!


To preface my choices, all of my preferences are dictated through the balance of convenience and satisfaction. This list will be based off of what I have near me.

The list is affected heavily by my travel time to these locations, I don't want to drive home with cold food!

I currently am obsessed with burgers so the top spot should not surprise you!

  1. Wayback Burger

    2. I have not enjoyed burgers in a long time, and just beef in general. As of right now I just have that craving.


  2. Sheslea's Pizza & Pasta

    3. That Mediterranean Pizza is the best pizza I have ever had personally.


  3. King of Spicy

    4. One of my all time favorite foods is Indian food, especially some Vegetable Tikka Misala.


  4. Osaka Japanese Cuisine

    5. I love Japanese food, it's main selling point is all of the steamed vegetables.


  5. Mint Hill Rock Store BBQ

    6. BBQ is just one of those things I will randomly get a craving for, and its is so distinct that it deserves a spot on this list.
